VISION

Our vision is to make agriculture better and attractive in Africa.

MISSIONS

Indus-Mega Farms is a community dedicated to lifting farmers out of poverty by rebranding and connecting them to mega farms where they can make more income.

We aim to create a working zero-waste farming system in Africa by bring easy market accessibility to the fingertips of many small holder farmers.

Indus-Mega Farms also aims to build a working funding system by connecting investors from different Nigerian Universities to pre-existing mega farms.
